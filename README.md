# 🔐 Phishing Email Detection & Awareness System

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on identifying phishing emails and spreading awareness about common cyber threats. Phishing attacks are one of the most frequent methods used by attackers to steal sensitive information such as passwords, banking details, and OTPs.

In this project, multiple phishing email samples were analyzed, and their characteristics were studied to help users understand how to detect and avoid such attacks.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Analyze phishing email samples
* Identify phishing indicators
* Classify email risks
* Educate users about phishing attacks
* Provide prevention guidelines

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

* Manual Email Analysis
* Email Header Analysis Tools
* Web Browser (URL Inspection)
* Documentation (MS Word / PDF)

---

## 🔍 Methodology

1. Collected phishing email samples (bank, job, OTP, lottery scams)
2. Analyzed sender email addresses
3. Checked for suspicious links and domains
4. Identified social engineering techniques (urgency, fear, rewards)
5. Classified emails based on risk level
6. Documented findings in a structured report

---

## 📧 Email Analysis (Examples)

## ⚠️ Phishing Indicators

Common signs of phishing emails:

* Fake sender email addresses
* Suspicious or unknown links
* Urgent or threatening language
* Generic greetings (e.g., “Dear User”)
* Unrealistic offers

---

## 🛡️ Prevention Guidelines

* Always verify sender email address
* Avoid clicking unknown links
* Check URLs carefully before login
* Never share OTPs or passwords
* Use Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
* Report suspicious emails
